Privacy Policy

iOSCryptoApp does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

This app processes data locally on your device only. All cryptographic operations (encryption, decryption, signing) are performed locally. No data is sent to any server. Keys are stored securely in your device's Keychain.

Communications

The app uses secure network communications and standard encryption solely to enable the messaging service functionality. Message contents are not saved on our servers and are not accessible to the developer.

Usage Data

No analytics, tracking, or user profiling data is collected.

Third Parties

The app does not share data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Security

The app uses security technologies and encryption that comply with industry standards.

Changes

This policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be published on this page.
